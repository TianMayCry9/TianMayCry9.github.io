I am Tianxiang Xiao, a Master's student at the Kunming Institute of Zoology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Chao Zhang. 

My work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence, computational biology, and evolutionary neuroscience. I apply artificial intelligence and deep learning models to develop novel algorithms, and leverage single-cell and spatial transcriptomics to investigate the evolutionary mechanisms of the brain.
